# Primary Goal of Data Science

The primary goal of data science is to **extract meaningful insights and value from data to support better decision-making**.

At its core, data science turns raw, often messy data into something useful—whether that’s understanding patterns, predicting future outcomes, or guiding actions. It combines statistics, programming, and domain knowledge to answer real-world questions.

For example:

* A business might use data science to **predict customer churn**.
* A hospital might use it to **improve patient outcomes**.
* A government agency might use it to **detect fraud or optimize services**.

So while the tools and techniques can vary, the central aim stays the same:

**Transform data into knowledge that drives informed decisions and tangible results.**
